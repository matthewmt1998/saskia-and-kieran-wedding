Our Story

Saskia and Kieran first met in the back of a tank in honour of Sandra’s birthday back in August 2016. Saskia spent the whole ride talking at Kieran, whilst Kieran was incredibly hungover and pretended to listen. 10 years later and not much of that has changed. Over the last decade they’ve been through plenty: 3 years of a 100 mile separation, living with both sets of in-laws, a house bought, and the bright idea of a rescue German Shepherd. 7th August 2026, after 10 years (almost exactly) together, they thought it was about time to tie the knot!
